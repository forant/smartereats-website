Are Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers Healthy? (Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers vs Doritos)

Quick Answer

Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers are a reasonable snack option, scoring 7/10. Compared to Doritos, they have fewer calories and lower sodium.

Quick Verdict

  • Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers: 7/10
  • Doritos: 6/10

Winner: Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers — they offer a lighter option with real ingredients.

Why Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers Falls Short

  • Only 1g of protein per serving offers minimal satiety compared to other snack options.
  • Contains a small amount of sugar (0.2g), which while low, isn't contributing to fullness.
  • While low in sodium at 30mg, it may not provide enough flavor for some snack lovers.

How Doritos Compares

  • Doritos contain 10g of protein, which is substantial for a snack and can help with satiety.
  • With 150 calories per serving, they are slightly higher in calories than Triscuit.
  • However, they are fairly processed with a long list of ingredients, which might concern health-conscious consumers.

Best Choice Based on Your Goal

  • Weight loss: Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers — lower in calories.
  • Energy / satiety: Doritos — higher protein content.
  • Occasional treat: Doritos — more flavorful option.

Better Alternatives

  • Plain popcorn — a whole grain, high fiber snack.
  • Air-popped rice cakes — low-calorie and versatile.
  • Raw veggies with hummus — crunchy, satisfying, and nutritious.
  • Almonds — nutrient-dense with healthy fats and protein.
  • Greek yogurt — high in protein and filling.

Bottom Line

Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers provide a lighter, more natural snack option than Doritos. While they may lack protein and flavor punch, their real ingredients and lower calorie count make them a decent choice for mindful snacking.
