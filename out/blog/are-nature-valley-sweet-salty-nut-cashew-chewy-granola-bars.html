Are Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars Healthy?

Quick Answer

Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars can be a relatively healthy snack depending on your goals. They offer a reasonable balance of calories and nutrients but come with some sugar and processing concerns.

Nutrition Snapshot

Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars provide a moderate nutritional profile. Here are the key numbers:

  • Calories per serving: 160
  • Protein: 2g
  • Overall score: 7/10

What Makes Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars a Good Choice

  • The bars have 160 calories, making them a manageable snack without being too filling.
  • Cashews and oats provide a moderate nutrient density compared to some other snacks.
  • Contains 2g of protein, which can add a bit of satiety.

Potential Downsides

  • With 9g of total sugar, these bars could be a concern if you're watching your sugar intake, especially as a light snack.
  • They are fairly processed, containing ingredients like corn syrup and palm kernel oil.

How Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars Fits Different Goals

  • Weight loss / calorie control: Good fit if you’re looking for a low-calorie snack option at 160 calories.
  • High protein / muscle support: Less ideal if you need a high protein boost, as they only contain 2g of protein.
  • Energy / satiety / blood sugar stability: Watch out for the 9g of sugar, which might cause a quick energy spike followed by a crash.
  • Heart health / lower sodium / less processed eating: Less ideal since they contain processed ingredients like corn syrup.

Healthier or Better Alternatives

  • Plain Greek yogurt: Higher in protein for better muscle support.
  • Raw nuts: More filling per calorie and lower in sugar compared to the granola bars.
  • Fresh fruit: Less processed and lower in added sugars than Nature Valley bars.
  • Hard-boiled egg: Higher protein and less processed compared to Nature Valley bars.
  • Unsweetened oatmeal: More filling and lower in sugar.

Final Verdict

Whether Nature Valley Sweet & Salty Nut Cashew Chewy Granola Bars is a good choice depends on your goal, portion size, and what you're comparing it to. They can be a decent snack for those looking for something manageable, but if you’re cautious about sugar or processed ingredients, you might want to consider other options.
