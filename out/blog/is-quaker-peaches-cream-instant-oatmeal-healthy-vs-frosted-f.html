Quaker Peaches & Cream Instant Oatmeal scores 6/10, which indicates it has some benefits but also significant downsides. Compared to Frosted Flakes, it offers slightly better protein content and fiber, but both options have high sugar levels.

Quick Verdict

Best Choice Based on Your Goal

  • Energy / satiety: Quaker Peaches & Cream Instant Oatmeal offers slightly better satiety due to its protein and fiber.
