Healthy Drinks

Honest comparisons of sports drinks, juices, smoothies, and enhanced waters — sugar by sugar, calorie by calorie.

Liquid calories are the single biggest sugar source in most people's diets — and the one most often hidden behind 'natural' or 'enhanced' marketing. We compare every major drink against the obvious alternative (often a Coke) so you can see the actual sugar load.
