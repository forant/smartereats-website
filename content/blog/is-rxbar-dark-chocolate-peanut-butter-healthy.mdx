# Is RXBAR Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Healthy?

## Quick Answer
RXBAR Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter can be a relatively healthy snack depending on your nutritional goals and portion size. It offers a good amount of protein and fiber, but its calorie content is higher than some other snack options.

## Nutrition Snapshot
RXBAR Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter has a balanced nutritional profile with beneficial components. Here are the key figures:
- Calories per serving: 230
- Protein: 10g
- Fiber: 4g
- Sugar: 11g
- Overall score: 7/10

## What Makes RXBAR Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter a Good Choice
- Contains 10g of protein, providing good satiety, especially for a snack.
- Has 4g of fiber, which helps maintain fullness between meals.
- Compared to other energy bars, it has a reasonable sugar content of 11g.

## Potential Downsides
- Fairly processed, which may not appeal to those seeking whole foods.
- Calorie content is 230, which might be more than needed for a light snack.

## How RXBAR Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Fits Different Goals
- **Weight loss / calorie control:** Less ideal if you're aiming for a low-calorie snack, as it contains 230 calories.
- **High protein / muscle support:** Good fit if you need protein post-workout, providing 10g per bar.
- **Energy / satiety / blood sugar stability:** Good fit if you seek sustained energy; its protein and fiber can help keep you full.
- **Heart health / lower sodium / less processed eating:** Less ideal if you're focused on whole foods, as it is fairly processed.
