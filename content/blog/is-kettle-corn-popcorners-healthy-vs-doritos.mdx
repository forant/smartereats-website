# Is Kettle Corn Popcorners Healthy? (Kettle Corn Popcorners vs Doritos)

Kettle Corn Popcorners are often marketed as a healthier snack alternative, but how do they truly stack up against Doritos? Both snacks boast a score of 7/10, but their nutritional profiles reveal distinct tradeoffs that may influence your choice.

## Quick Answer
Kettle Corn Popcorners are a light snack with only 80 calories per serving, making them a reasonable choice. However, their very low protein content at 0.06g may leave you wanting more in terms of satiety compared to Doritos, which provide 10g of protein.

## Quick Verdict
- **Kettle Corn Popcorners:** 7/10
- **Doritos:** 7/10

Winner: neither — both snacks have their pros and cons, but neither stands out significantly in this comparison.

## Why Kettle Corn Popcorners Fall Short
- **Very low protein:** At only 0.06g, Kettle Corn Popcorners may not be satisfying enough to curb hunger.
- **Added sugar:** Contains some added sugar, which can accumulate if multiple servings are consumed.
- **Limited satiety:** While light at 80 calories, the low protein may lead to quicker hunger compared to higher-protein snacks.

## How Doritos Compares
- **Higher protein:** With 10g of protein, Doritos can provide better satiety than Kettle Corn Popcorners.
- **Moderate calories:** At 150 calories, Doritos offer more energy, though with a heavier feel.
- **Processing concerns:** Doritos are more processed with a longer ingredient list, which may be a drawback for some consumers.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — both options are similar in calories, but look for a more filling snack.
- **Energy / satiety:** Doritos — higher protein content helps keep hunger at bay.
- **Occasional treat:** Kettle Corn Popcorners — lighter and more snack-friendly for casual munching.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain popcorn: A whole grain option that's high in fiber and low in calories.
- Air-popped corn: Offers a satisfying crunch without added sugars or fats.
- Mixed nuts: Provide healthy fats and protein for better satiety.
- Greek yogurt: High in protein, perfect for a more filling snack.
- Fresh fruit: Naturally sweet and hydrating, great for a wholesome treat.

## Bottom Line
Kettle Corn Popcorners provide a light, low-calorie snack option, but their very low protein content may leave you feeling unsatisfied. With a score of 7/10, they don't significantly outperform Doritos, which offer more protein but come with processing tradeoffs. For a truly filling snack, consider alternatives like mixed nuts or Greek yogurt.
