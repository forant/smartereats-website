# Are Kettle Corn Popcorners Healthy? (Kettle Corn Popcorners vs Doritos)

## Quick Answer
Kettle Corn Popcorners are a reasonable snack choice, but they lack protein and are low in satiety compared to Doritos. Their light calorie count is balanced by low protein and some added sugar.

## Quick Verdict
- Kettle Corn Popcorners: 7/10
- Doritos: 7/10

Winner: neither — both snacks have their downsides.

## Why Kettle Corn Popcorners Falls Short
- Very low in protein at 0.06g, which may not be very satiating.
- Contains some added sugar, which can add up if eating multiple servings.
- While low in calories at 80, they may not keep you full for long.
- Low sodium content at 50mg is a plus, but it doesn't offset the other downsides.

## How Doritos Compares
- Doritos have 10g of protein, significantly aiding in satiety compared to Kettle Corn Popcorners.
- Moderate calorie count at 150 provides energy without being overly heavy.
- More processed with a longer ingredient list, which may be a concern for some.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — pick a real alternative.
- **Energy / satiety:** Doritos offer better satiety due to higher protein content.
- **Occasional treat:** Kettle Corn Popcorners can work for a light snack, but be mindful of added sugar.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain popcorn for a low-calorie, high-fiber option.
- Air-popped popcorn to avoid added sugars and fats.
- Vegetable sticks with hummus for a nutritious crunch.
- Nuts for healthy fats and protein that keep you full.
- Greek yogurt for a protein-rich snack option.

Kettle Corn Popcorners may seem like a healthier snack, but their low protein and added sugars limit their effectiveness for satiety. Consider alternatives for a more balanced snack.
