# Is Junkless Granola Bar Healthy? (Junkless Granola Bar vs Snickers)

## Quick Answer
The Junkless Granola Bar is a decent snack with a score of 7/10, offering 130 calories and less sugar than Snickers. However, its modest protein and fiber might not satisfy your hunger for long.

## Quick Verdict
- Junkless Granola Bar: 7/10
- Snickers: 6/10

Winner: Junkless Granola Bar — it has fewer calories and less sugar, making it a lighter snack option.

## Why Junkless Granola Bar Falls Short
- Ingredients are somewhat processed, including brown rice syrup and chocolate drizzle, which may not suit everyone's preference for whole foods.
- The bar has modest protein (2g) and fiber, which means it may not keep you full as long as other higher-protein snacks.
- While it has 7g of sugar, that's still enough to keep it relatively light compared to many similar snacks.

## How Snickers Compares
- Snickers contains 24g of sugar, significantly higher than the Junkless Granola Bar, which could lead to a quick energy spike followed by a crash.
- It has 4g of protein, providing slightly more satiety than the Junkless Granola Bar, but still lacks significant fiber.
- The ingredients are highly processed with corn syrup and artificial flavors, making it less appealing for those seeking whole food options.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Junkless Granola Bar is a better option due to its lower calorie count.
- **Energy / satiety:** Neither — both may not keep you full for long.
- **Occasional treat:** Snickers can be enjoyed, but it’s not the best choice for regular snacking.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain Greek yogurt: High in protein and keeps you full longer.
- Whole orange: Offers natural sweetness along with fiber.
- Plain oatmeal: A filling option rich in fiber and nutrients.
- Hard-boiled egg: Packed with protein and low in calories.
- Unsweetened sparkling water: A refreshing, calorie-free drink option.

## Bottom Line
The Junkless Granola Bar provides a lighter, lower-sugar alternative to many snacks, but it may not be as satisfying as higher-protein options. While it’s a step above ultra-processed candies like Snickers, it still has room for improvement in terms of satiety.
