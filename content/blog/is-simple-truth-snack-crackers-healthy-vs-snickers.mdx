Simple Truth Snack Crackers score 7/10. They provide 150 calories for moderate energy but have trade-offs in protein and sodium. In contrast, Snickers scores 6/10, primarily due to its higher sugar content.

## Quick Verdict
- **Simple Truth Snack Crackers:** 7/10

## How Snickers Compares
