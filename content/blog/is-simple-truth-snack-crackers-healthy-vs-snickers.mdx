# Are Simple Truth Snack Crackers Healthy? (Simple Truth Snack Crackers vs Snickers)

## Quick Answer
Simple Truth Snack Crackers are a reasonable choice for a light snack but have trade-offs in satiety and sodium. Compared to Snickers, they have less sugar but may not keep you full for long.

## Quick Verdict
- Simple Truth Snack Crackers: 7/10
- Snickers: 6/10

Winner: Simple Truth Snack Crackers — they provide moderate energy with less sugar.

## Why Simple Truth Snack Crackers Falls Short
- Only 2g of protein and 1g of fiber, which might not keep you full for long.
- 320mg of sodium is on the higher side for a light snack.

## How Snickers Compares
- Snickers contains 4g of protein, which is relatively good for a chocolate bar and can help with satiety.
- Snickers has 24g of total sugars, which is on the higher side for a light snack.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Simple Truth Snack Crackers — lower in sugar.
- **Energy / satiety:** Simple Truth Snack Crackers — moderate energy boost.
- **Occasional treat:** Snickers — higher protein but also high in sugar.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain Greek yogurt — high in protein and low in sugar.
- Whole orange — natural sugars and high in fiber.
- Raw almonds — healthy fats and protein for satiety.
- Air-popped popcorn — low-calorie and high in fiber.
- Baby carrots — crunchy, low-calorie snack with vitamins.

Simple Truth Snack Crackers are a decent choice for a light snack, especially if you're looking to reduce sugar intake. However, their low protein and fiber content may leave you feeling hungry sooner.
