# Is Fruifuls Healthy? (Fruifuls vs Snickers)

## Quick Answer
Fruifuls has a score of 5/10, indicating it may not be a satisfying snack due to its high sugar content and lack of protein. Compared to Snickers, Fruifuls contains less sugar but also offers no protein for satiety.

## Quick Verdict
- Fruifuls: 5/10
- Snickers: 6/10

Winner: Snickers — it provides protein, which may aid in satiety.

## Why Fruifuls Falls Short
- Contains **9g of sugar** without any protein, which might not offer much satiety.
- Highly processed with ingredients like 'invert sugar' and 'natural flavors', which might not be ideal for a balanced snack.
- Low calorie count makes it a light snack option, providing **50 calories** per serving, but this could lead to quick hunger.

## How Snickers Compares

- Both snacks are relatively processed, with Snickers including corn syrup and artificial flavors.
