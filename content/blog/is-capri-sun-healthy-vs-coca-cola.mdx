# Is Capri-Sun Healthy? (Capri-Sun vs Coca-Cola)

Many parents wonder about the healthiness of popular kids' drinks like Capri-Sun. Given its marketing, one might expect it to be a healthier choice. However, when compared to Coca-Cola, the nutritional value tells a different story.

## Quick Answer  
Capri-Sun scores 5/10, primarily due to its high sodium content of 400 mg. While it contains no sugar, its lack of protein and fiber limits its filling potential, similar to Coca-Cola, which also scores 5/10 for similar reasons.

## Quick Verdict
- **Capri-Sun:** 5/10  
- **Coca-Cola:** 5/10  

Winner: neither — both drinks lack protein and fiber, making them less satiating.

## Why Capri-Sun Falls Short
- High sodium content at 400 mg could be a concern for frequent consumption.
- Barely any protein means it won’t fill you up.
- As a liquid without fiber, it may not be very filling.
- Although low in calories at 34 per serving, the lack of other nutrients limits its value as a snack.

## How Coca-Cola Compares
- Coca-Cola has 10.6g total sugar per serving, which can lead to quick energy but doesn’t keep hunger at bay.
- Like Capri-Sun, Coca-Cola lacks protein and fiber, which are essential for satiety.
- Coca-Cola's 43 calories per 100ml makes it a low-calorie option, but it still doesn’t provide lasting satisfaction.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — both are low in nutrients and may not support weight loss.
- **Energy / satiety:** neither — both lack protein and fiber for sustained energy.
- **Occasional treat:** neither — both are light options but fail to provide nutritional benefits.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain water: Hydrating with no calories or sugars.
- Coconut water: Offers some electrolytes and natural sugars.
- Unsweetened iced tea: A light beverage with zero calories.
- Low-fat yogurt: Provides protein and probiotics for gut health.
- Fresh fruit: Naturally sweet and packed with vitamins and fiber.
