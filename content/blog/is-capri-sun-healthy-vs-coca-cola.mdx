# Is Capri-Sun Healthy? (Capri-Sun vs Coca-Cola)

## Quick Answer
Capri-Sun sits at 5/10, mainly due to its high sodium content at 400 mg. While it has low calories, it lacks protein and fiber, making it less filling compared to Coca-Cola, which also scores 5/10.

## Quick Verdict
- Capri-Sun: 5/10
- Coca-Cola: 5/10

Winner: neither — both have similar shortcomings in nutrition.

## Why Capri-Sun Falls Short
- High sodium content at 400 mg could be a concern for frequent consumption.
- Barely any protein to help with satiety.
- As a liquid without fiber, it may not be very filling.

## How Coca-Cola Compares
- Coca-Cola also has high sugar, with 10.6g total sugar per serving, contributing to low satiety.
- Both beverages offer low calorie counts, with Coca-Cola at 43 calories per 100ml.
- Like Capri-Sun, Coca-Cola lacks protein and fiber, making both options less effective for hunger management.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — both are low in calories but high in sodium and sugar.
- **Energy / satiety:** neither — both lack protein and fiber for lasting fullness.
- **Occasional treat:** neither — both are sugary drinks with little nutritional value.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain water — hydrating and calorie-free.
- Unsweetened iced tea — low calories and refreshing with no added sugars.
- Sparkling water — bubbly and zero calories with added flavor options.
- Fresh fruit — naturally sweet, hydrating, and packed with fiber.
- Greek yogurt — high in protein and satisfying.
