# Is Green Goodness Smoothie Healthy?

## Quick Answer
Green Goodness Smoothie is not healthy due to its extremely high sugar content and calorie count. At 198g of sugar, it's more than most snacks should contain, especially in liquid form.

## Quick Verdict
- Green Goodness Smoothie: 4/10

## Why Green Goodness Smoothie Falls Short
- 198g sugar hit: This is very high for a snack.
- 1080 calories: Surprisingly high for a snack, making it easy to overconsume calories without feeling full.
- Liquid form: Less filling than solid snacks, leading to potential overeating.
