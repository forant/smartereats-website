# Are Kickin' Cheddar Corn Nuts Healthy? (Kickin' Cheddar Corn Nuts vs Doritos)

## Quick Answer
Kickin' Cheddar Corn Nuts are not a particularly healthy snack choice, especially when compared to Doritos. While they have a moderate calorie count, their low protein and high processing raise concerns.

## Quick Verdict
- Kickin' Cheddar Corn Nuts: 6/10
- Doritos: 7/10

Winner: Doritos — they offer more protein and lower sugar.
