# Is Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores Healthy? (Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores vs Snickers)

Many people choose granola bars thinking they're a healthier snack option. Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores claims to be a nutritious treat, but how does it stack up against the classic Snickers? This comparison uncovers some interesting facts about both options.

## Quick Answer
Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores scores 7/10, thanks to its notable fiber content and low calorie count. In contrast, Snickers scores 6/10, primarily due to its high sugar content and calorie density.

## Quick Verdict
- **Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores:** 7/10
- **Snickers:** 6/10

Winner: Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores — it offers better fiber and fewer calories, making it a lighter snack.

## Why Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores Falls Short
- Only 1g of protein, which is lower than some more filling snack options.
- Moderate sugar content at 6g total, typical for a granola bar but worth noting.
- While low in calories (90 calories), it may not satisfy hunger as effectively as higher protein options.

## How Snickers Compares
- Snickers contains 24g of sugar, which is significantly higher than the granola bar.
- Offers 4g of protein, which can aid in feeling full compared to the granola bar.
- Snickers has a higher calorie count at 210 calories, making it easier to overconsume.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores is a better option.
- **Energy / satiety:** Snickers may offer more immediate satisfaction due to higher protein, but it's less healthy overall.
- **Occasional treat:** Quaker Chewy Granola Bar S’mores is lighter, making it a more sensible treat choice.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain Greek yogurt: High in protein and low in sugar.
- Fresh fruit like an apple: Naturally sweet, high in fiber, and low in calories.
- Hard-boiled eggs: Great source of protein and very filling.
- Raw nuts: Healthy fats and protein for satiety.
