# Is Pop Corners Sea Salt Healthy? (Pop Corners Sea Salt vs Doritos)

Consumers often seek snacks that seem healthier, like Pop Corners Sea Salt. Despite its marketing, a deeper look shows some tradeoffs. Comparing it to Doritos reveals important insights about its nutritional profile.

## Quick Answer
Pop Corners Sea Salt scores 7/10, making it a reasonable snack option with moderate calories at 210. It has decent protein but falls short on fiber and sodium. Doritos scores slightly lower at 6/10, being more processed but offering higher protein.

## Quick Verdict
- **Pop Corners Sea Salt:** 7/10

- Offers only 4g of protein, which is decent but not as filling as some alternatives.
- Features 1g of fiber, making it less filling compared to other snacks.
- Sodium content is relatively high at 330mg, which could be a concern for some consumers.

## How Doritos Compares
