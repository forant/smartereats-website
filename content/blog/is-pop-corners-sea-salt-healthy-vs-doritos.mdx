# Are Pop Corners Sea Salt Healthy? (Pop Corners Sea Salt vs Doritos)

## Quick Answer
Yes, Pop Corners Sea Salt is relatively healthy, scoring 7/10. Compared to Doritos, it has moderate protein and simpler ingredients, but less fiber.

## Quick Verdict
- Pop Corners Sea Salt: 7/10
- Doritos: 6/10

Winner: Pop Corners Sea Salt — it has better ingredients and moderate protein.

## Why Pop Corners Sea Salt Falls Short
- While flavorful, the sea salt version might not offer as much fiber (1.0g) as some might prefer for satiety.
- Calorie density is 210 calories per 50g, which could add up if portion control is not observed.
- 330mg sodium isn't excessive for a snack but should still be monitored.

## How Doritos Compares
- Doritos have 10g of protein, which is quite high for a snack chip, potentially aiding in satiety.
- They contain only 150 calories per 28g serving, making them a more calorie-dense option.
- High levels of processing and ingredient additives are typical for Doritos, which may be a concern for some consumers.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Pop Corners Sea Salt — lower calorie density is beneficial.
- **Energy / satiety:** Pop Corners Sea Salt — moderate protein helps with fullness.
- **Occasional treat:** Doritos — better for a snack but should be limited.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain popcorn — a high-fiber, low-calorie snack option.
- Rice cakes — light and versatile for toppings, lower in calories.
- Air-popped corn — a healthier popcorn alternative with no added fats.
