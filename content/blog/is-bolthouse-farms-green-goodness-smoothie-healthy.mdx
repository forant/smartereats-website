# Is Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie Healthy?

## Quick Answer
Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie is a mixed bag — fine for occasional use, but not ideal for regular snacking. Its high sugar and calorie content can be a concern for many.

## Nutrition Snapshot
Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie has a profile that includes a significant amount of sugar and calories. Here are the key numbers:
- Total sugar: 198g
- Calories: 1080
- Fiber: 13.5g
- Overall score: 5/10

## What Makes Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie a Good Choice
- Contains 13.5g of fiber, which can aid in satiety between meals.
- Includes a variety of fruits and vegetables, such as spinach and broccoli, suggesting a wholesome ingredient list.

## Potential Downsides
- Contains 198g of total sugar, which is quite high and typical of smoothies.
- High calorie content at 1080 calories, which may be more filling than needed for a snack and can lead to overconsumption.
- Sugar overload that can impact overall daily intake.
