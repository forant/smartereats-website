# Is BelVita Breakfast Biscuit (Cinnamon Oat) Healthy?

## Quick Answer
BelVita Breakfast Biscuit (Cinnamon Oat) is a mixed bag — fine for fiber content, but not ideal for sugar control. It can work as a convenient breakfast option if you manage portion sizes.

## Nutrition Snapshot
BelVita Breakfast Biscuit (Cinnamon Oat) offers a decent nutritional profile, particularly with its fiber content. Here are key numbers to consider:
- Calories per serving: 401 kcal per 100g
- Protein: 6.01g
- Fiber: 8.02g
- Sugar: 18.04g
- Overall score: 7/10

## What Makes BelVita Breakfast Biscuit (Cinnamon Oat) a Good Choice
- Provides 8.02g of fiber, which helps with moderate satiety.
- Contains 6.01g of protein, aiding in keeping hunger at bay until the next meal.
- Made with whole grains like cracked buckwheat and rye flakes, adding nutritional value.

## Potential Downsides
- Total sugar content is 18.04g, which is higher than typical for a light breakfast snack.
- Includes multiple forms of sugar, such as raisin paste, brown sugar, and molasses, which can spike blood sugar levels.
- Calorie density at 401 kcal per 100g might lead to consuming more calories than intended if not mindful of portion sizes.
