Garden Veggie Straws Sour Cream & Chives is not particularly healthy, scoring 5/10, mainly due to low protein and fiber. Compared to Doritos, it offers less satiety and nutrients.

## Quick Verdict
- Garden Veggie Straws Sour Cream & Chives: 5/10

Winner: Doritos — while both snacks have shortcomings, Doritos provide more protein.
