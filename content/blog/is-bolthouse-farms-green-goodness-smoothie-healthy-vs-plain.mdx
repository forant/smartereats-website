Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie rates a 5/10 for healthiness, primarily due to its high calorie and sugar content. In comparison, Plain Greek Yogurt scores 8/10, offering more protein with significantly less sugar.

## Quick Verdict
- Bolthouse Farms Green Goodness Smoothie: 5/10
- Plain Greek Yogurt: 8/10

Winner: Plain Greek Yogurt — it provides better nutrition for a snack.

- At 1080 calories for the bottle, it's quite calorie-dense for a snack, which might lead to overeating.

- Contains empty calories, which do not provide lasting energy.

- Offers 15g of protein, which provides good satiety for a snack.
- Contains only 6g of total sugars, significantly lower than the Green Goodness Smoothie, reducing sugar load.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
