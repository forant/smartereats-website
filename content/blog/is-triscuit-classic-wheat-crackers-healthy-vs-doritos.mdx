# Is Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers Healthy? (Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers vs Doritos)

Many snack options claim to be healthy, but how do Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers stack up against Doritos? While Triscuit promotes a health halo with real ingredients, it’s essential to look closely at what they offer.

## Quick Answer
Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers score 7/10, helped by only 90 calories per serving, making them a relatively light snack option. They have a minimal ingredient list but offer low protein, impacting satiety compared to Doritos, which have higher protein but are more processed.

## Quick Verdict
- **Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers**: 7/10
- **Doritos**: 6/10

Winner: Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers — they provide a healthier alternative with lower calories and real ingredients.

## Why Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers Falls Short
- Only 1g of protein per serving offers minimal satiety compared to other snack options.
- Contains a small amount of sugar (0.2g), which, while low, isn't contributing to fullness.
- Although low in sodium at 30mg per serving, the protein content limits overall satisfaction.

## How Doritos Compares
- Doritos contain 10g of protein, which is substantial for a snack and can help with satiety.
- With 150 calories per serving, they are relatively light but offer more calories than Triscuit.
- However, they are ultra-processed with a long list of ingredients, raising concerns for those focused on quality.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Triscuit Classic Wheat Crackers are a lighter option.
- **Energy / satiety:** Doritos may provide more fullness due to higher protein.
- **Occasional treat:** Neither — pick a real alternative.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain popcorn: A whole grain snack that's low in calories and provides fiber.
- Hummus with veggies: Offers protein and fiber for better satiety.
- Almonds: Nutrient-dense with healthy fats and protein.
- Rice cakes: A low-calorie, crunchy alternative for snacking.
- Greek yogurt: High in protein and can be a satisfying snack.
