# Is Ritz Crackers Healthy? (Ritz Crackers vs Doritos)

## Quick Answer
Ritz Crackers score 6/10, indicating they are a moderate snack option but lack in protein and are highly processed. Compared to Doritos, they offer similar caloric content but fall short in nutritional benefits.

## Quick Verdict
- Ritz Crackers: 6/10
- Doritos: 6/10

Winner: neither — both lack significant nutritional benefits.

## Why Ritz Crackers Falls Short
- They contain only 2.31 grams of protein, which is low for providing satiety.
- Higher level of processing with ingredients like palm oil and glucose-fructose syrup.
- With 145.2 calories per serving, Ritz Crackers provide a moderate caloric intake for a snack.
- The sodium content is reasonable at 160 mg, but still noteworthy for those monitoring their intake.

## How Doritos Compares
- Doritos contain 10g of protein per serving, which can aid in feeling fuller longer.
- They have 150 calories per serving, slightly higher than Ritz Crackers.
- Both snacks are highly processed, with Doritos also using vegetable oils and corn syrup solids.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — both are calorie-dense without substantial nutrients.
- **Energy / satiety:** neither — Ritz offers low protein, while Doritos are processed.
- **Occasional treat:** neither — choose less processed snacks for better nutrition.
