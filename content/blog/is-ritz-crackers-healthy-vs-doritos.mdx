# Is Ritz Crackers Healthy? (Ritz Crackers vs Doritos)

Consumers often view Ritz Crackers as a convenient snack option, but is that perception accurate? This comparison with Doritos reveals some surprising trade-offs in nutrition and processing.

## Quick Answer
Ritz Crackers score 6/10, offering a moderate caloric intake with 145.2 calories per serving but lack sufficient protein for satiety. While the sodium level is reasonable, the high level of processing and low protein content are concerns.

## Quick Verdict
- **Ritz Crackers**: 6/10
- **Doritos**: 6/10

Winner: neither — both snacks are similar in scores but have significant nutritional drawbacks.

## Why Ritz Crackers Falls Short
- They contain only **2.31 grams of protein**, which is low for providing satiety.
- Higher level of processing with ingredients like **palm oil and glucose-fructose syrup**.
- Although they have **145.2 calories per serving**, they may not keep you full for long.

## How Doritos Compares
- **Doritos** contain **10g of protein** per serving, which can aid in feeling fuller longer.
- They have **1g of fiber**, contributing modestly to satiety, while Ritz Crackers lack fiber.
- Both snacks are highly processed, with Doritos using ingredients like **vegetable oils and corn syrup solids**.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — both options are calorie-dense and low in nutrients.
- **Energy / satiety:** neither — low protein and fiber in both snacks can lead to hunger soon after.
- **Occasional treat:** neither — both snacks are highly processed and lack nutritional balance.

## Better Alternatives
- **Plain popcorn** — a whole grain snack that's low in calories and high in fiber.
- **Air-popped rice cakes** — light and crunchy, offering a healthier base for toppings.
- **Veggie sticks with hummus** — nutritious and satisfying, packed with fiber and protein.
- **Greek yogurt** — rich in protein and can be paired with fruits for added flavor.
- **Apple slices with almond butter** — a wholesome, filling snack combining protein and fiber.

Ritz Crackers are convenient but score only 6/10 due to low protein and high processing. For a more satisfying and healthier snack, opt for alternatives like plain popcorn or veggie sticks with hummus.
