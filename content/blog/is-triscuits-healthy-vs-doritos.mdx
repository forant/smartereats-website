# Is Triscuits Healthy? (Triscuits vs Doritos)

## Quick Answer
Triscuits score a solid 7/10, thanks to their fiber content and real ingredients. However, they only provide 3g of protein, which may not keep you full for long compared to Doritos, which also score 7/10 but offer more protein.

## Quick Verdict
- Triscuits: 7/10
- Doritos: 7/10

Winner: neither — both have tradeoffs in nutrition.

## Why Triscuits Falls Short
- Provides only 3g of protein, which might not keep you full for very long.
- Contains 1g of sugar, including added sugar that may not be ideal for frequent snacking.
- Includes some level of processing with added oils and flavorings, though typical for crackers.

## How Doritos Compares
- Doritos offer 10g of protein per serving, promoting better satiety than Triscuits.
- They have 0g of sugar, making them a favorable option for those watching their sugar intake.
- However, they are highly processed with ingredients like vegetable oils and corn syrup solids.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** neither — pick a real alternative.
- **Energy / satiety:** Doritos — better protein content.
- **Occasional treat:** Triscuits — more whole ingredients.

## Better Alternatives
- Plain popcorn: a whole grain snack that's high in fiber.
- Carrot sticks: a crunchy, low-calorie option loaded with nutrients.
- Hummus with veggies: provides healthy fats and protein.
- Plain Greek yogurt: packed with protein and probiotics.
- Unsweetened applesauce: a low-calorie, naturally sweet snack.

Triscuits are a reasonable snack with some fiber and real ingredients, but their low protein may leave you wanting more. Doritos, while higher in protein, are more processed. Neither is ideal for regular consumption, so explore healthier alternatives.
