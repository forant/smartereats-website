# Is Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal Healthy? (Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal vs Frosted Flakes)

## Quick Answer
Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal is healthy, scoring 7/10, thanks to its higher protein and fiber content compared to Frosted Flakes. While it has some sugar, it still offers more satiety benefits.

## Quick Verdict
- Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal: 7/10
- Frosted Flakes: 6/10

Winner: Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal — it provides more protein and fiber, aiding in satiety.

## Why Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal Falls Short
- Contains 10g of sugar, which might be higher than ideal for a light snack.
- Includes processed ingredients like brown rice syrup and canola oil, though not extreme for a cereal.
- Offers 10g of protein, which is higher than average, but can still be improved for those seeking more.
- Reasonable sodium level at 130mg, lower than many processed cereals.

## How Frosted Flakes Compares
- Frosted Flakes contain 12.65g total sugar, making it a sugar overload compared to Kashi.
- Offers only 2.11g of protein, which is quite low and may not sustain hunger well.
- Convenient and quick, but lacks the nutritional benefits found in Kashi Go.

- **Energy / satiety:** Kashi Go Peanut Butter Crunch Cereal — higher protein supports fullness.
