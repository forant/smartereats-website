The MadeGood Organic Chewy Granola Bar is a reasonably healthy snack option, scoring 7/10. Compared to Snickers, it has lower sugar and calories, but also offers less protein.

## Quick Verdict
- MadeGood Organic Chewy Granola Bar: 7/10
