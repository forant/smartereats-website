# Is Naked Juice Mango Machine Healthy? (Naked Juice Mango Machine vs Coca-Cola)

Consumers often perceive Naked Juice Mango Machine as a healthier choice. However, its sugar content and protein levels tell a different story. While marketed as a nutritious beverage, it may not satisfy hunger as effectively as solid snacks.

## Quick Answer
Naked Juice Mango Machine scores 6/10, primarily due to its low calorie content of 60 per serving. However, it contains 12.9g of sugar without adequate protein or fiber, making it less satisfying compared to Coca-Cola, which scores 4/10 and also lacks filling nutrients.

## Quick Verdict
- Naked Juice Mango Machine: 6/10
- Coca-Cola: 4/10

Winner: Naked Juice Mango Machine — it has lower calories, but high sugar limits its benefits.

## Why Naked Juice Mango Machine Falls Short
- Contains 12.9g of sugar, which can lead to a sugar overload.
- Only 0.3g of protein fails to help with satiety.
- Low fiber content (1.05g) further reduces fullness.
- Being a liquid snack may not satisfy hunger as effectively as solid foods.

## How Coca-Cola Compares
- Coca-Cola has 10.6g of sugar per 100ml, slightly less than Naked Juice Mango Machine.
- Like Mango Machine, Coca-Cola lacks protein and fiber, making it unfulfilling.
- However, Coca-Cola is refreshing and offers a quick sugar boost, which can be energizing.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Neither — both have high sugar and low satiety.
- **Energy / satiety:** Neither — both fail to provide lasting fullness.
- **Occasional treat:** Coca-Cola — it serves better as an infrequent indulgence.
