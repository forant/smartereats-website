# Is Naked Juice Mango Machine Healthy? (Naked Juice Mango Machine vs Coca-Cola)

## Quick Answer
Naked Juice Mango Machine is not particularly healthy due to its high sugar content and low protein. Compared to Coca-Cola, it offers slightly more nutritional benefits but still has significant drawbacks.

## Quick Verdict
- Naked Juice Mango Machine: 6/10
- Coca-Cola: 4/10

Winner: Naked Juice Mango Machine — it has lower calories and more natural ingredients, despite the sugar.

## Why Naked Juice Mango Machine Falls Short
- Relatively high sugar content at 12.9g per serving, contributing to a sugar overload.
- Contains only 0.3g of protein, which means it won’t fill you up effectively.
- The liquid form may not be as filling as solid snacks due to low fiber (1.05g).

## How Coca-Cola Compares
- Coca-Cola has a sugar content of 10.6g per 100ml, which is still high but slightly less than Naked Juice.
- Both drinks are low in calories, with Coca-Cola at 43 calories per 100ml, making them both light options.
- Like Naked Juice, Coca-Cola lacks protein and fiber, offering little in terms of satiety.

## Best Choice Based on Your Goal
- **Weight loss:** Neither — pick a real alternative.
- **Energy / satiety:** Neither — both are low in protein and fiber.
- **Occasional treat:** Neither — consider moderation for both.
